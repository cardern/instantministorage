About Us

Experience

IMS has over 15 years of experience serving commercial and industrial businesses in the Greater Wisconsin and Illinois areas. We are familiar with the needs for storage and can provide you with the best possible solution to your problems. IMS has both WDBE and SBE certifications.

Flexibility

Our mission is helping you succeed, and that requires flexibility. IMS can offer you multiple sizes and colors of modular containers for short or long-term rental, lease, or purchase, and we are able to deliver directly on-site.

Rentals

IMS is able to offer both short and long-term rentals and can accommodate seasonal storage needs. We can deliver as many units as you need exactly when you need them.

Facts:

  • Durable: Vented heavy duty steel with lift pockets. Pockets are perfect for industrial and manufacturing use
  • Modular: Units are at ground level which reduces the risk of industrial accidents and decreases loading and unloading time
  • Secure: Double doors on each unit have four locking bars to ensure the contents remain safe
  • Multiple color options: midrange brown, grey or blue, with custom colors available

Multiple sizes are available:

  • 10'L x 8'W x 8' 6"H
  • 20'L x 8'W x 8' 6"H
  • 40'L x 8'W x 8' 6"H
  • 40'L x 8'W x 9' 6"H
